--> Gradient Color   <--
-- It is a Css3 Function 
-- It has 2 types : linear-gradient() - radial-gradient()
-- Don't Use background-color: -- Use background:
--Syntax : background: linear-gradient(direction, colour-stop1, colour-stop2, ...);
* direction : to bottom (Default value) to top - to left - to right -to bottom right - to bottom left-to top right- to top left - Angle :30deg , 180deg , 1turn , 2turn
* color : red , #ff0 , hsl() ...
* stop : 20% , 30% , 70% stop color x and start color y
